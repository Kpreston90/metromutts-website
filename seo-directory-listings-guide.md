## Tier 1: High-Priority Directories (Do These First)

These have the highest domain authority and direct impact on local search rankings.

### 1. Google Business Profile
- **URL:** https://business.google.com
- **Cost:** Free
- **Why:** The single most important listing for local SEO. Directly affects Google Maps and local pack rankings.
- **Action:** Claim/verify your listing. Add 10+ photos, respond to all reviews, post weekly updates, add all services with descriptions.

### 2. Yelp
- **URL:** https://biz.yelp.com
- **Cost:** Free (paid options available)
- **Why:** DA 94. Yelp listings frequently appear in top 3 Google results for "dog boarding Tulsa."

### 3. Facebook Business Page

- **Cost:** Free
- **Why:** DA 96. Social signal + directory listing. Appears in search results.

- **Why:** Powers Siri and Apple Maps searches. Growing market share.

### 5. Bing Places

## Key Tips for Maximum SEO Impact

1. **NAP Consistency** — Use the exact same business name, address, and phone number on every single listing. Even small differences (St. vs Street, Suite vs Ste.) can confuse Google.

2. **Link to specific pages** — Where possible, link to your Boarding page (metromutts.com/boarding) or Grooming page (metromutts.com/grooming) instead of just the homepage. This passes authority directly to your target ranking pages.

3. **Photos matter** — Upload 5–10 high-quality photos to every listing. Listings with photos get 42% more direction requests and 35% more click-throughs.

4. **Reviews are fuel** — After every positive experience, ask customers to leave a review on Google first, then Yelp. Aim for 50+ Google reviews within 6 months.

5. **Monitor for duplicates** — Search for your business name on each platform before creating a new listing. Duplicate listings hurt rankings.

6. **Category selection** — Always select the most specific category available (e.g., "Dog Boarding Kennel" over "Pet Services").
